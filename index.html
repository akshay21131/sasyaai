Crop Disease Detection

Upload a photo of any leaf and our MobileNetV2 CNN model will instantly identify the disease, its cause, and the treatment steps.

Computer Vision · CNN · TensorFlow
🌍

Soil & Fertilizer Advisor

Enter your soil's NPK values, pH, temperature and rainfall. Our Random Forest model recommends the best crop and fertilizer — including organic alternatives.

Machine Learning · Random Forest
